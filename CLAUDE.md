This directory contains reference privacy policies from 37signal, Tinybeans and FamilyAlbum
in markdown format. Others will be added. You can find refernces in ./refernces. The privacy policy will be in a file name like pp-legal.md under each dir. When planning draft versions of privacy policies, use these as structural
references. Our site is a private family photo-sharing app with no ads, no tracking,
and no AI/ML training on photos.

Some action item notes from our team:

1.  Define and publish a data ownership & portability commitment
    One plain-language page: who owns photos, what happens if you shut down, whether you will ever sell to another company.

2.  "Why we're permanent" statement
    Address the Lifecake scenario directly. Articulate what structural guarantees you offer around data portability, platform continuity, and user ownership of their memories.

3.  Copy tonality to avoid clichés
    "Precious moments," "privacy-first," "safe space" is expected in this category. We need 2–3 brand language choices specific to our actual differentiators and repeat them consistently.
