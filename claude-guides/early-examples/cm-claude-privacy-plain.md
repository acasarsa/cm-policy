# Collectable Moments — Privacy Policy (Plain Language)
*Last updated: April 2026*

---

At Collectable Moments, your family's privacy is the whole point. This site exists so families can share photos of their kids privately — without social media, without algorithms, and without anyone else getting their hands on your photos.

Here's a plain-language breakdown of how we handle your data.

---

## Is Collectable Moments safe?

Yes. Everything on this site is private by design.

- Your photos can only be seen by people you personally invite.
- Nothing is ever made public.
- We don't run ads. We don't sell your data. Ever.
- We do not use your photos — or your children's photos — for AI training, facial recognition, or any machine learning purpose. Full stop.
- We have no interest in tracking you or building a profile on you.

---

## Who runs this?

Collectable Moments is currently operated by an individual. We plan to transition to an LLC in the future. When that happens, we'll update this policy.

---

## What information do you need from me to sign up?

To create an account you'll need to provide:
- Your name
- Your email address
- A password

That's it. We ask for the minimum we need to get you set up.

---

## What other data do you collect?

When you use the site, we may automatically collect some basic technical information — things like your IP address, browser type, and device info. We use this only to keep the site running securely and to prevent abuse. We do not use third-party analytics tools to track your behavior on the site.

---

## Do you use cookies?

We use only the cookies necessary to keep you logged in and to make the site function. We do not use tracking cookies or any third-party cookies for advertising or analytics.

---

## Who can see my photos?

Only the people you invite. Period. We do not view your photos except in rare cases where a technical issue requires manual intervention — and only with your permission. No one else at Collectable Moments looks at your content.

---

## What about photos of children?

Collectable Moments is built specifically with children's privacy in mind. Here's what that means:

- **We will never use photos of children for AI training, facial recognition, or any automated image processing.**
- Account holders must be 13 or older.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover this has happened, we will delete that information immediately.
- The children in your photos are not users of this site — they have no account and their information is not collected.
