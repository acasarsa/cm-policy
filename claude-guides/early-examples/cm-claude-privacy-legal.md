## 2. What We Collect and Why

Our guiding principle is to collect only what is necessary to operate the Service.

### 2a. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored in hashed form). We use this information to authenticate you, communicate with you about your account, and provide access to the Service.

### 2b. Photos and Media

Photos and videos you upload are stored on our servers and are accessible only to you and the individuals you explicitly invite. We do not access, analyze, or process your photos or videos for any purpose other than storing and delivering them to you and your invited users.

**We do not use photos or videos — including photos of minors — for AI training, machine learning, facial recognition, or any automated image analysis. This prohibition is absolute.**

### 2c. Technical Data

We may collect technical information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers. This data is used solely for security, fraud prevention, and to maintain the integrity of the Service.

### 2d. Communications

If you contact us for support, we retain that correspondence to provide assistance and maintain a support history.

## 6. Children's Privacy and Protection of Minors

Collectable Moments is a platform designed for adults to share photos of their families, including children. We take the privacy of minors extremely seriously.

- **Account holders must be at least 13 years of age.** We do not knowingly create accounts for individuals under 13.
- If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.
- Children who appear in photos uploaded to the Service are subjects, not users. We collect no personal information from or about them directly.
- **Under no circumstances will photos of minors be used for AI training, facial recognition, automated tagging, or any form of machine learning.** This prohibition applies to all third-party service providers as well.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at [CONTACT EMAIL].

This Service is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## 7. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We disclose information only in the following limited circumstances:

**a. To invited users you designate.** When you invite another person to view your album or group, they will have access to the photos and media you have shared with them, as well as basic account identifiers (e.g., your display name).

**b. To our service providers.** As described in Section 4, we share data with infrastructure, email, and payment providers solely to operate the Service.

**c. When required by law.** We will disclose user data only when compelled by a valid legal process — including a warrant, subpoena, or court order issued by a court of competent jurisdiction. It is our policy to notify affected users of such requests unless we are legally prohibited from doing so or an emergency circumstance prevents it.

**d. To protect the Service.** We may disclose information where necessary to investigate or prevent fraud, abuse, or violations of this policy or our Terms of Service.

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

- Secure cloud infrastructure with access controls
- Regular security reviews

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

## 9. Content Visibility and Public Access

No content on Collectable Moments is publicly accessible. All photos, videos, and albums are private by default. There are no public profiles, no public feeds, and no mechanism by which content can be made publicly visible.

- **Legal holds:** We may retain data longer than the above periods if required by applicable law.
